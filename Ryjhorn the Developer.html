About

Hello my name is Ryjhorn Weekes and I am a participant on the Black Codher Programme.

I applied to be on the BlackCodher Programme when I heard about the opportunity through word of mouth. I saw it for the amazing opportunity that it was. It is chance to further expand my technological skills and to execute a career move into a world of Full Stack Web Development.

This website will showcase the projects that I will be working on for the duration of my time on the programme as well as the personal projects I will take on. Happy Browsing Fellow Coders!

What is Black CodHer Bootcamp?


This Programme has been pioneered by Coding Black Females & Niyo Enterprise for women that want to begin their tech career.
Niyo Enterprise and Coding Black Females have partnered together to put on a 6-month Coding Bootcamp programme in the West Midlands. It is designed to empower 50 women of colour over 18, who are either unemployed, or are employed with a salary below Â£20k per annum; the aim being to train them to become Full Stack Web Developers. The Programme is an economic empowerment initiative seeking to propel all participants into professional development roles on completion.
This course is highly intensive, learners are required to do homework to optimize engagement with the course content, students are also
expected to produce a portfolio with various projects across different languanges, in preparation for new employment prospects.
